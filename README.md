# OSHA Severe Injury & Compliance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on processing, cleaning, and analyzing over 84,000 rows of federal safety data from the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA). The goal is to transform large-scale raw data into an executive-ready interactive dashboard to identify corporate compliance risks and high-risk industrial sectors.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methods
- **Data Cleaning (Python & Google Colab):** Managed missing values, handled structural inconsistencies, formatted data types, and filtered high-severity attributes.
- **Data Deployment & Visualization (Spreadsheet / Advanced Excel):** Developed a dynamic dashboard using interconnected Pivot Tables, automated KPI Cards, and dynamic Dual-Slicers.

## 📊 Key Insights Captured
1. **High Severity Rate:** Out of 30,571 logged safety incidents, there were 32,806 total hospitalizations, indicating a critical need for immediate corrective action in high-risk operational sectors.
2. **Top Enforcement Penalty:** United States Postal Service represents the highest compliance risk with over $6.1M in total OSHA penalties, followed closely by heavy industrial manufacturing (Republic Steel) at $2.8M.
3. **Targeted Safety Training:** Logistics, Warehousing, and Retail sectors dominate the hospitalization chart. This insight justifies that safety training should heavily focus on manual handling safety and warehouse traffic management.
